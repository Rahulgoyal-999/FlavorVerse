French Silk Pie Bars

A decadent dessert combining the rich flavors of French silk pie in a convenient bar format

Description

These French Silk Pie Bars are a delightful twist on the classic French silk pie. They feature a buttery chocolate cookie crust topped with a smooth, creamy chocolate filling and finished with a layer of whipped cream. Perfect for parties or as a special treat, these bars are rich, decadent, and absolutely irresistible.

Ingredients

  • For the crust:
    • 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
    • 6 tablespoons melted butter
    • 1/4 cup sugar
  • For the filling:
    • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 cup powdered sugar
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • For the topping:
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Chocolate shavings for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix the cookie crumbs, melted butter, and sugar until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into the prepared pan and bake for 10-12 minutes. Let cool completely.
  4. Melt the chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl, stirring until smooth. Let cool slightly.
  5. In a large bowl, beat the butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy.
  6. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  7. Stir in the melted chocolate and vanilla extract.
  8. Spread the filling over the cooled crust and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  9. Before serving, whip the heavy cream with powdered sugar and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  10. Spread the whipped cream over the filling and garnish with chocolate shavings.
  11. Cut into bars and serve.

Tips & Notes

  • Use high-quality chocolate for the best flavor.
  • Make sure the butter is properly softened before beating.
  • For food safety, use pasteurized eggs or egg substitute.
  • Store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
