Scallion Pancake with Eggs

Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Total: 35 mins
Servings: 2

About this Recipe

A delicious fusion dish that combines the crispy, flaky texture of Chinese scallion pancakes with the rich, creamy addition of eggs. This recipe creates a perfect breakfast or brunch dish that's both satisfying and flavorful. The combination of green onions and eggs creates a beautiful harmony of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup hot water
1/2 cup chopped green onions
4 large eggs
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions

1

In a large bowl, mix flour and hot water until a shaggy dough forms. Knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.

2

Divide the dough into 4 equal portions. Roll each portion into a thin circle.

3

Brush each circle with sesame oil and sprinkle with chopped green onions.

4

Roll up each circle tightly and then coil it into a spiral. Flatten the spiral into a pancake.

5

Heat vegetable oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Cook each pancake until golden brown on both sides.

6

In a separate bowl, beat eggs with salt and pepper.

7

Pour beaten eggs over the cooked pancakes and cook until eggs are set.

8

Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
