Scallion-Roll

Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 25 mins
Total: 45 mins
Servings: 6

About this Recipe

A delicious and flaky scallion roll that's perfect for any meal. These rolls are made with a simple dough and filled with fresh scallions, creating a savory treat that's both beautiful and delicious. Serve them warm with your favorite soup or curry.

Ingredients

3 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp active dry yeast
1 tsp salt
1 cup warm water
2 tbsp olive oil
2 cups chopped scallions
1/4 cup sesame oil
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 egg, beaten (for brushing)

Instructions

1

In a large bowl, combine flour, yeast, and salt. Mix well.

2

Add warm water and olive oil to the flour mixture. Stir until a dough forms.

3

Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

4

Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

5

While the dough is rising, prepare the filling by mixing chopped scallions with sesame oil and black pepper.

6

Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 6 equal portions.

7

Roll each portion into a thin rectangle, spread with the scallion filling, and roll up tightly.

8

Place the rolls on a greased baking sheet, cover, and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

9

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Brush the rolls with beaten egg.

10

Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.
