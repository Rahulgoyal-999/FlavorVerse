Vegan Sheet Pan Fajitas

A delicious and healthy vegan version of classic fajitas, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8-10 tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Optional toppings: avocado, salsa, vegan sour cream

Prep Time

20 minutes

Cook Time

30 minutes

Servings

4

Category

Vegan

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Press the tofu for at least 30 minutes to remove excess water, then cube it.
  3. In a large bowl, combine olive oil, soy sauce, lime juice, garlic, and spices.
  4. Add tofu, peppers, onion, and mushrooms to the bowl and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Spread the mixture on a large sheet pan in a single layer.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, stirring halfway through, until vegetables are tender and tofu is crispy.
  7. Warm the tortillas in the oven or on a skillet.
  8. Serve the fajita mixture in warm tortillas with your favorite toppings.
