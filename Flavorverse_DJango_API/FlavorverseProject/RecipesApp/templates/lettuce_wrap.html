Lettuce Wrap

A healthy and delicious Asian-inspired dish that's perfect for a light meal or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground chicken or turkey
  • 1 head of lettuce (iceberg or butter lettuce)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 cup water chestnuts, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Prep Time

15 minutes

Cook Time

20 minutes

Servings

4

Category

Appetizer

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add ground chicken/turkey and cook until browned, breaking it into small pieces.
  4. Add soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and rice vinegar. Stir well.
  5. Add water chestnuts and green onions. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Separate lettuce leaves and wash them thoroughly.
  8. Serve the meat mixture with lettuce leaves for wrapping.
