Lemon Crumb Bars

A tangy, sweet dessert with a buttery crumb crust and zesty lemon filling

Description

These Lemon Crumb Bars are a delightful combination of a buttery shortbread crust, a tangy lemon filling, and a crumbly topping. The perfect balance of sweet and tart makes these bars an irresistible treat for lemon lovers. They're easy to make and perfect for potlucks, bake sales, or as a refreshing dessert after any meal.

Ingredients

  • For the crust and crumb topping:
    • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 cup cold butter, cut into small pieces
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • For the lemon filling:
    • 4 large eggs
    • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
    • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice (about 2-3 lemons)
    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • For garnish (optional):
    • Powdered sugar
    • Lemon zest

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine 2 1/4 cups flour, 1 cup sugar, and salt.
  3. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Press about 2/3 of the crumb mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  6. Bake the crust for 15 minutes, then remove from the oven.
  7. While the crust is baking, prepare the lemon filling by whisking together the eggs, sugar, lemon juice, flour, lemon zest, and salt until smooth.
  8. Pour the lemon filling over the hot crust.
  9. Sprinkle the remaining crumb mixture over the filling.
  10. Bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set and the top is lightly golden.
  11. Allow to cool completely in the pan before cutting into bars.
  12. Dust with powdered sugar and garnish with lemon zest if desired.

Tips & Notes

  • Use fresh lemon juice for the best flavor.
  • Make sure the butter is cold when making the crust for a flaky texture.
  • Allow the bars to cool completely before cutting to ensure clean slices.
  • Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
  • For a more intense lemon flavor, increase the lemon zest to 2 tablespoons.
