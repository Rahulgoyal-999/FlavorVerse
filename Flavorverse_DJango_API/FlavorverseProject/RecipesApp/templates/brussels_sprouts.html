Brussels Sprouts with Gochujang Butter

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Total: 35 mins
Servings: 4

About this Recipe

A delicious side dish that transforms humble Brussels sprouts into a flavor-packed sensation. The combination of roasted Brussels sprouts with a spicy, savory gochujang butter creates a perfect balance of heat and umami. This dish is sure to convert even the most skeptical Brussels sprouts eaters.

Ingredients

1 lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
3 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
2 tbsp gochujang paste
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp sesame seeds, toasted (for garnish)

Instructions

1

Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.

2

In a large bowl, toss the Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, and pepper until well coated.

3

Spread the Brussels sprouts in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet, cut-side down.

4

Roast for 20-25 minutes, until the sprouts are tender and the cut sides are caramelized.

5

While the Brussels sprouts are roasting, prepare the gochujang butter: In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, gochujang paste, honey, soy sauce, and minced garlic until well combined.

6

Once the Brussels sprouts are done roasting, transfer them to a serving bowl.

7

Add the gochujang butter to the hot Brussels sprouts and toss until the butter melts and coats the sprouts.

8

Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds and serve immediately.
