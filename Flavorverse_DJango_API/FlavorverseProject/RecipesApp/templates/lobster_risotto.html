Lobster Risotto

A luxurious Italian dish featuring tender lobster and creamy Arborio rice

Description

This Lobster Risotto is a sophisticated dish that combines the rich, sweet flavor of lobster with the creamy texture of perfectly cooked Arborio rice. The dish is enhanced with white wine, shallots, and a touch of saffron, creating a luxurious dining experience perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • For the lobster:
    • 2 (1 1/2-pound) live lobsters
    • 4 cups water
    • 1 tablespoon salt
  • For the risotto:
    • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
    • 1/2 cup dry white wine
    • 4 cups lobster stock (from cooking lobsters)
    • 2 shallots, finely chopped
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/4 teaspoon saffron threads
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For garnish:
    • Fresh parsley, chopped
    • Lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. Cook the lobsters in salted water for 8-10 minutes. Remove and let cool.
  2. Remove the meat from the shells and reserve the shells for stock.
  3. Simmer the shells in water for 20 minutes to make stock. Strain and keep warm.
  4. In a large pan, saut√© shallots and garlic in butter until translucent.
  5. Add the rice and toast for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly.
  6. Pour in the wine and cook until absorbed.
  7. Add warm stock one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until absorbed.
  8. When rice is almost done, add the saffron and cream.
  9. Stir in the Parmesan cheese and lobster meat.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Garnish with parsley and serve with lemon wedges.

Tips & Notes

  • Use only fresh, live lobsters for the best flavor.
  • Keep the stock warm while adding to the risotto.
  • Stir constantly to release the rice's starch for creaminess.
  • Don't overcook the rice - it should be al dente.
  • Add the lobster meat at the end to prevent overcooking.
