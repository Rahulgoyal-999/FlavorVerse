1. Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  2. Whisk eggs and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Fold in chocolate mixture, flour, and vanilla.
  4. Pour into ramekins and bake for 12 minutes.
  5. Serve warm with ice cream.

2. Classic Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 200g mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 10 ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until creamy.
  2. Fold in mascarpone cheese.
  3. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer with mascarpone.
  4. Chill for 4 hours, then dust with cocoa powder.
  5. Serve chilled.

3. Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 200g cream cheese
  • 1 cup crushed biscuits
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup fresh strawberries
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Mix crushed biscuits and butter, press into a cake tin.
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and heavy cream.
  3. Spread over the base and refrigerate for 4 hours.
  4. Top with fresh strawberries and serve.

4. Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee.
  2. Add milk to form a soft dough, shape into balls.
  3. Deep fry until golden and soak in sugar syrup.
  4. Serve warm.

5. Mango Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mango puree
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp gelatin
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Heat mango puree, milk, and sugar.
  2. Dissolve gelatin in warm water and add to the mixture.
  3. Pour into molds and refrigerate for 4 hours.
  4. Serve chilled.
