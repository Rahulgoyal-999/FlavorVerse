1. Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & chili powder to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add tomato puree and cook until thick.
  3. Add spices and cream, then stir well.
  4. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan or rice.

2. Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 2 tbsp ghee

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice and keep aside.
  2. Sauté onions, garlic, and spices in ghee.
  3. Add vegetables and cook until soft.
  4. Mix with rice and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve with raita.

3. Chana Masala

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpeas (boiled)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices, then cook until thick.
  3. Add chickpeas and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with rice or chapati.

4. Aloo Paratha

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2 potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee

Instructions:

  1. Mix mashed potatoes with spices.
  2. Stuff in rolled-out dough and seal.
  3. Roll again and cook on a hot tawa.
  4. Apply ghee and serve hot with curd.

5. Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 bunch spinach (blanched & pureed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and sauté onions and garlic.
  2. Add spinach puree and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in cream and spices.
  4. Add paneer cubes and simmer.
  5. Serve with roti or rice.

6. Dal Tadka

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (toor dal)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil lentils until soft.
  2. Heat ghee and sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes.
  3. Add spices and mix well.
  4. Combine with lentils and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve with rice or roti.
