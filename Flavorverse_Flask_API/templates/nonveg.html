1. Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (boneless, cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, turmeric, and ginger-garlic paste. Let it rest for 1 hour.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until golden brown.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in cream, garam masala, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan or rice.

2. Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp ghee

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and biryani masala.
  2. Fry onions in ghee until golden brown. Add marinated chicken and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Parboil rice and layer it over the chicken mixture.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 15 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

3. Mutton Rogan Josh

Ingredients:

  • 500g mutton
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add mutton, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in yogurt and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Add garam masala, cover, and cook until tender.
  5. Serve with steamed rice or naan.

4. Fish Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g fish fillet
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and add mustard seeds.
  2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in turmeric, curry powder, and coconut milk.
  4. Add fish and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice.

5. Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (leg pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp tandoori masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp garlic paste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, tandoori masala, lemon juice, and garlic paste.
  2. Let it rest for at least 2 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C and bake for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney.

6. Prawn Masala

Ingredients:

  • 500g prawns
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic paste, turmeric, and red chili powder.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until thick.
  4. Stir in prawns and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
