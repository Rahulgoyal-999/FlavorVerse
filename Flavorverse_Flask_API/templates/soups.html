1. Classic Tomato Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Blend the soup until smooth.
  5. Stir in cream, salt, and pepper. Serve hot.

2. Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • 200g cooked chicken (shredded)
  • 1 carrot (chopped)
  • 1 celery stalk (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 100g egg noodles
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot and sauté onions, carrots, and celery.
  2. Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Add noodles and cook until tender.
  4. Stir in shredded chicken and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve warm with bread.

3. Creamy Mushroom Soup

Ingredients:

  • 200g mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Melt butter in a pot and sauté onions, garlic, and mushrooms.
  2. Add vegetable broth and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Blend the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in cream, salt, and pepper.
  5. Serve hot with croutons.

4. Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lentils (washed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 carrot (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil and sauté onions, garlic, and carrots.
  2. Add lentils and vegetable broth. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Blend half of the soup for a creamy texture.
  4. Stir in cumin, salt, and pepper.
  5. Serve warm with a slice of bread.

5. French Onion Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 onions (thinly sliced)
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 baguette (sliced)
  • 1 cup grated cheese

Instructions:

  1. Melt butter in a pan and caramelize onions with sugar.
  2. Add white wine and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in beef broth and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Toast baguette slices with grated cheese.
  5. Serve soup with cheesy toast.

6. Thai Coconut Soup

Ingredients:

  • 200g chicken (sliced)
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 chili (sliced)
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions:

  1. Heat chicken broth and add ginger and chili.
  2. Add sliced chicken and cook until done.
  3. Stir in coconut milk and fish sauce.
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add lime juice and serve warm.
